Questions you all have asked.

Neil Armstrong took a first step by moonwalking. A Mars Rover is a special research robot in Mars. ISS stands for International Space Station. We use Telescopes to see planets and stars because stars and some planets are far away that we cannot see them with the unaided eye. A black hole is a celestial body which is beyond Neutron Star Stage.

Saturn is a gas giant which has rings. Jupiter is the largest and the first of four Jovian planets. Neptune is the farthest planet. Pallas is a type of an asteroid. Ceres is the first dwarf planet located in Asteroid Belt. Uranus is a seventh planet and also the coldest. Pleiades is an open star cluster close to Earth also known as Seven Sisters. Orion Nebula is the closest Nebula to the Earth,it contains 2,000 more matter than the sun.
